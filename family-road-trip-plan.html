Route Logic

Do the hardest northbound drive before NYC, then use the fixed Lancaster stay as the recovery base after Manhattan.

Philadelphia is possible only as a swap on June 13. The better family plan is Amish Country plus Landis Valley, because Hersheypark already uses the main Pennsylvania park day.

After DC, overnight near Roanoke/Christiansburg so June 16 is long but reasonable.

Optional Adds

9/11 Museum would likely add about $140-$170. A NYC observatory would likely add $230-$330. Philadelphia adds parking/tolls/time even though Independence Hall itself is low-cost.

Savings Levers

Pack breakfasts on road days, skip the observatory, use the Airbnb for groceries, cap Hersheypark extras, and avoid taxis except arrival/departure with luggage.

Risk Items

NYC food, hotel parking, Hersheypark food, ticket fees, and Northeast tolls are the easiest lines to underestimate. The $800 cushion is realistic, not padding for souvenirs.
